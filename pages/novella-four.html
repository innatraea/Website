Novella Four: Haitasi

A farm boy faces the My'yh's judgment…

What happens when kindness is the only power that matters?

"Some places hold such ancient power that every step within shapes destiny." — Tavid the Traveler

Jonaas Al'Shane never sought greatness. Raised on a quiet farm in Aliselle Falls, he dreamed only of seeing Innatraea—until the truth of his birth shattered everything. Now he stands before the My'yh, an ancient judge who has turned countless souls to stone. Her question will cut to the core of who he is.

Beyond her temple lies Haitasi—a realm outside of time—and the hidden city of Dryumiri, where the Shedon dwell. An ancient Sacred Folk race whose emotions flow visible beneath their skin. A people haunted by a slow extinction they cannot escape. They have been waiting for centuries, isolated.

Jonaas doesn't know what he is, or what he's becoming. But the Shedon see something in this quiet farm boy. Something impossible. Something that could change everything.

If Haitasi doesn't take it all from him first.

Step through the ancient stone archway with Jonaas and discover what it truly means to be seen.
