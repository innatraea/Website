Innatraea: An Epic Fantasy Saga of Destiny, Sacrifice, and Love

"Every legend begins with the small footsteps of ordinary Innatraeans." — Tavid the Traveler

Step into Innatraea, a world where magic shapes fate and women — Weavers, warriors, matriarchs, and queens — stand at the center of power. Across diverse kingdoms and ancient realms, sentient mythical creatures walk alongside humanity, cultures clash and intertwine, and secrets buried for generations rise to change everything.

At its heart are three childhood friends from the quiet farm town of Aliselle Falls, each called to a different destiny:

Rosalie Sharone, a Weaver prodigy of unmatched power, journeys to join an ancient order that could make her the most powerful woman in Innatraea — or destroy everything she loves.

Ariela Marelda, a warrior born to the sea, must navigate treacherous waters of politics and prejudice as she fights to prove herself in a world that sees her as less than human.

Jonaas Al'Shane, a simple farm boy with a hidden past, discovers that the truth of his birth could reshape the fate of an ancient, dying race — if it doesn't kill him first.

Their paths diverge across kingdoms, oceans, and realms beyond time itself. But destiny has woven their fates together, and what they discover will challenge everything they believe about magic, sacrifice, and what it truly means to love.
